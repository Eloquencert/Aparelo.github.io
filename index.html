Aparelo is a new Ukrainian travel agency that has great prospects. Our tour agency works with the following European countries: Germany, France, Slovakia, Poland, Portugal, Great Britain, Spain, the Czech Republic, Norway, Sweden, Switzerland, but in our perspectives there are even more countries.


When planning a vacation, it is very important to trust real professionals: the outcome of the trip depends entirely on the service.


Today, in order to go on a trip to European countries, tourists need little. You only need to have a biometric passport and entrust the processing of the package of documents to a reliable agency.


Thanks to this, tours to Europe have become a fairly common type of tourism for those seeking new experiences. At the same time, it does not matter what you want: tour operators are ready to offer you a full range of possible tours. Whether you plan a weekend trip to get to know a new city, or you prefer a ten-day tour to see everything - the choice depends only on you.
